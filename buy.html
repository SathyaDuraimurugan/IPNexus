Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know the IP is legitimate?

All sellers on IPMarket undergo a thorough verification process, and each listed IP is reviewed by our team. We also provide documentation verification services and escrow protection to ensure authenticity. For added security, you can request additional verification or engage our partner attorneys for legal due diligence.

What types of licenses are available?

IPMarket supports various licensing arrangements including exclusive licenses, non-exclusive licenses, territory-specific rights, field-of-use restrictions, and full ownership transfers. Each listing specifies the available options, and many sellers are open to negotiating custom terms to meet your specific needs.

How does the payment process work?

We use a secure escrow system to protect both buyers and sellers. When you agree to purchase, funds are held in escrow until you receive and approve the IP assets. For larger transactions, we can arrange milestone-based payments. We support credit cards, wire transfers, and several cryptocurrency options.

Can I request customizations to the IP?

Yes, many sellers offer customization services to adapt their IP to your specific requirements. You can discuss customization needs through our secure messaging system, and any agreements will be documented in the final contract. Additional fees may apply based on the scope of customization.

What if I'm not satisfied with my purchase?

Our escrow system ensures you can review the IP before funds are released. If the IP doesn't match the description or lacks promised functionality, you can dispute the transaction. Our resolution team will review the case and may provide refunds or require sellers to remedy issues. This is why it's important to thoroughly review all documentation before approving the transfer.
